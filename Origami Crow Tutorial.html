Origami Crow Tutorial


This poster was initially a challenge to design in that all thirty-two steps needed to fold an origami crow had to fit on a tabloid-size poster while still remaining understandable. Text had to be kept to a minimum, and I was limited to black, white, and a single additional color.

I relied on the gestural quality of the arrows and drawn folds in the paper to convey the steps, and overall this was a very fun project to experiment with, particularly for information design.
