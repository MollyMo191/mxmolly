Opia


The focus of this project was to identify a problem inspired by the concept of speculative design, and then develop a solution to the issue. The problem I chose to focus on is that individuals who use assistive vision devices, such as eyeglasses and contact lenses, need to have a more accessible way to have their prescriptions updated, because the current system is time-consuming and cost-inefficient for many people.

Over the course of six weeks, I developed Opia, an eyeglasses brand that uses nanoparticle-embedded lenses in combination with a dual scanner and computer system to automatically detect the status of the eyes and adjust the prescription of the lenses to best benefit the wearer.
