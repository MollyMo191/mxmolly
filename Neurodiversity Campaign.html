Neurodiversity Campaign


The goal for this project was to create a campaign about a topic close to me, and I chose neurodivergency. The word "neurodivergent" is used to describe people who are not of the typical neuro-typeâ€”those who are autistic, have ADHD, Tourettes, etc.; personality disorders are also sometimes included in definitions of neurodiversity. Many people in my life are neurodivergent, and I wanted to create a social campaign to inform people about the topic while sourcing the information directly from neurodivergent individuals.

I designed a poster advertising the campaign, a booklet featuring content from three live interviews, three digitally-painted portraits of those who I interviewed, a tutorial about how to create a stim toy, and a batch of stim toys themselves which were handed out on the day of presentation.
