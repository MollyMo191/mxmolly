Breaking Bread Cookbook


Various materials for the Breaking Bread in McLean County Long Table event.

I designed an email invitation for the Long Table event using existing branding from the 2021 Breaking Bread in McLean County lecture and interview online series, in which members of various migrant communities across Bloomington-Normal shared stories and recipes from their families and cultures.

Using information gathered from these beautiful videos, I designed a commemorative cookbook that highlights the recipes from the lecture series. There are over twenty recipes across sixty-four pages. Copies of the cookbook were sold to the public at the McLean County Museum of History in Bloomington, Illinois.
